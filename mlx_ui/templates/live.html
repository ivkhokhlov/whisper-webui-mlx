Current beta scope

The experimental surface stays narrow on purpose:

  1. Capture microphone audio from the browser with localhost permissions.
  2. Send short chunks to the Parakeet streaming backend and show partial text here.
  3. Keep batch Queue separate until live capture proves stable enough to save sessions.
